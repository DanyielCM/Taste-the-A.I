Taste the 20's:
Spongy chocolate cake with
LA LECHERA®
Difficulty
Servings
Preparation
Challenging
8
120min
INGREDIENTS
1/2 cup of sunflower oil (100 g)
1/4 cup of white sugar (50 g)
3 eggs (150g)
1/2 teaspoon of vanilla essence (2 g)
1 ½ cups LA LECHERA® CONDENSED MILK (250 g)
1/2 cup of wheat flour (100 g)
1/2 teaspoon of baking powder (2 g)
1 pinch of baking soda (1 g)
2 tablespoons of cocoa powder (30 g)
1/2 teaspoon of sea salt (2 g)
1 tablespoon of chocolate sauce (20 g)
4 tablespoons of Swiss meringue (40 g)
1 tablespoon of orange sauce (20 g)
1 tablespoon of crushed peanuts (15 g)
2 tablespoons of blueberries (30 g)
Let's cook!
STEP 1
1. Prepare 4 round molds of 10 cm in diameter with release agent on the sides and parchment paper on the base.
STEP 2
2. Preheat oven to 170˚C.
STEP 3
3. In a medium bowl, mix the oil, sugar, eggs, vanilla essence and LA LECHERA ® CONDENSED MILK
STEP 4
4. In another bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, baking soda, cocoa, and salt.
STEP 5
5. Slowly pour the dry ingredients over the liquid ingredients. Stir until completely integrated.
STEP 6
6. Distribute the preparation in the molds and bake for approximately 30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean.
STEP 7
7. Let the cakes warm on a rack, then unmold and remove the paper from the base. Let cool completely.
STEP 8
8. Take each cake and remove the top with the help of a serrated knife to leave it neat.
DECORATE
9. For the assembly, pour a little chocolate sauce on a plate and place a cake on it. Arrange a flake of meringue on the surface of the cake and with the help of a blowtorch, lightly toast it. Then place a little of the orange sauce in the center of the meringue and on it a little of the crushed peanuts. Finish your decoration with a few fresh blueberries on the plate.
Taste the 60's:
Apple and walnut blondie
with LA LECHERA®
Difficulty
Servings
Preparation
Intermediate
16
90min
Ingredients
2 small green apples, peeled (240 g)
1/2 cup of real white chocolate (80 g)
1/2 cup of Brazil nuts (75 g)
1 1/4 cups of butter (250 g)
2 cups of LA LECHERA® CONDENSED MILK (400 g)
1/2 cup of brown sugar (140 g)
2 AA eggs (100 g)
1 teaspoon of vanilla essence (4 g)
1 cup of wheat flour (220 g)
1 teaspoon of baking powder (4 g)
1 pinch of sea salt (2 g)
meringues or sighs
comestible flowers
Let's cook!
STEP 1
1. Prepare a 25*25 cm square mold with release agent on the sides and parchment paper on the base.
STEP 2
2. Cut the apple into cubes of approximately 1 centimeter and reserve them. Finely chop the white chocolate and the nuts, set them aside as well.
STEP 3
3. Preheat oven to 170˚C.
STEP 4
4. Melt the butter in a pan until it has no lumps, make sure it is not too hot.
STEP 5
5. Add the LA LECHERA® CONDENSED MILK and sugar to the butter; mix very well.
STEP 6
6. Add the eggs to the previous mixture along with the essence of vanilla and mix until well integrated.
STEP 7
7. In another bowl, mix the dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, and salt.
STEP 8
8. Slowly pour the dry ingredients over the liquid ingredients, stirring until fully integrated. Finally, add the apple cubes, the chocolate and the chopped nuts. Mix very well.
STEP 9
9. Pour the preparation into the mold and bake for approximately 45 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
STEP 10
10. Let the cake warm on a rack, unmold and remove the paper from the base. Once it is completely cool, smooth the edges and cut it into ten squares.
DECORATE
11. Crush the meringue with your hands and cover the surface of each square with the pieces. To finish, decorate with edible flower petals.
Taste the 70's:
Creamy passion fruit with
LA LECHERA®
Difficulty
Servings
Preparation
Challenging
12
180min
Ingredients
1 teaspoon of unflavored gelatin (4 g)
1 tablespoon of water (20g)
1 cup LA LECHERA® CONDENSED MILK
3/4 cup of passion fruit juice (70 g)
1 tablespoon lemon juice (18 g)
2 units of egg yolk (37 g)
2 teaspoons of corn starch (7 g)
1 heaping tablespoon of sugar (20 g)
1 1/2 cups NESTLÉ® MILK CREAM (140 g)
Semi-sweet chocolate coverage (200 g)
1/2 cup of crushed meringue (60 g)
1/2 cup Kiwi, Mango Lulo chopped into small pieces
4 tablespoons of whipped cream
1/2 Cup of Edible Flowers
Let's cook!
STEP 1
1. In a small bowl, put the water and add the flavorless gelatin. Mix and let hydrate for 10 minutes.
STEP 2
2. In a small saucepan, pour the LA LECHERA® CONDENSED MILK, passion fruit juice, lemon juice, egg yolk, starch and sugar. Mix very well.
STEP 3
3. Take the preparation to cook over medium heat stirring constantly.
STEP 4
4. When the mixture boils, let cook for 40 more seconds. Remove from the heat and immediately pass through the strainer.
STEP 5
5. Pour the preparation on a can and cover with vinyl paper in contact. Refrigerate until completely cold.
STEP 6
6. Once the passion fruit cream is cold, beat it with the help of the mixer. Add the milk cream and beat until integrated.
STEP 7
7. Pour the preparation into half-sphere silicone molds with a diameter of 5 cm. Take to freeze to be able to unmold easily.
STEP 8: Chocolate capsule
8. Melt dark chocolate coverage in a bain-marie. Take a 7cm round silicone mold and paint the entire cavity with the chocolate. Take to refrigerate for 5 minutes, remove from the fridge and repeat the same process two more times. Let the chocolate crystallize completely and unmold.
STEP 9: Assemble and decorate
9. At the base of each chocolate capsule, put a thin layer of crushed meringue and cover it with a little whipped cream. Unmold the creamy passion fruit and put it on the bed of whipped cream. Decorate with pieces of fruit and edible flower petals.
