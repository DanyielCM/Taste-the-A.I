For 100 years in Colombia, la Lechera’s Condensed Milk has mixed all the traditional ingredients to prepare all the humanly possible dessert recipes in the world. To celebrate its 100 years in the country, we created 10 NEVER-BEFORE-SEEN dessert recipes, each dessert representing a decade in history. To make this a reality, we mixed never
before-use ingredients.
